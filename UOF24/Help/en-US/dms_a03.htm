How to download and score the document

Select documents you want to read from the document directory, then select to download for reading.

文件文件下載與觀看

In the left menu of document center, click “Directory” ,  “Category” ,  “Author” ,  “Full text search” and “Commonly used documents” to select documents to be downloaded.

若需要知道文件的詳細資料，在顯示方式選擇“詳細資料”；若想要查看無權限讀取或是作廢的文件，請勾選“顯示其他狀態文件(勾選後，會依不同角色增加顯示以下狀態的文件；目錄管理者、作者：已下架、已作廢、未生效、已失效；讀者：已下架)”；
文件可以依文件編號、文件名稱、作者、版本、狀態、公佈日或關鍵字來排序。

Click on the title of the document to be downloaded, you can then select to view document or save.

 

Rank comments

Before ranking comments, it is needed to be downloaded for reading; documents not yet downloaded cannot be scored.

One version can only be scored once, and opinion must be proposed for the scoring.
