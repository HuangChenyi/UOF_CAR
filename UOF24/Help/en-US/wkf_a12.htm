Import and export the form

Convert related information such as form content and flow into xml file for exporting and importing.

Importing form: Click first the name of the form to be imported, then click “Import rev.” to select the xml file of the form to do the form importing. And the imported form category and form name should be the same. After the importing, a non-released form version will then appear. You can use form design to change the form or to release.
Exporting form: Click first the form name, then click “Export form” on the form version to be exported, then enter the file name to be saved and press save.
