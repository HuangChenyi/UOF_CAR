Query customer data

It provides the possibility to query customer data from different angles, and acquire the query results quickly.

Query according to category

Query customer data by customer category

Click ¡§category¡¨ label on the screen at the left side, the lower left side will then display tree nodes of all customer categories.

According to the selected category node, the screen on the right side will display released customer data and newly added unreleased customer data list under such category.

Note: Released customer data displayed on the screen are customer data that an individual has authority to browse.

Query by industry

Query customer data according to industry type

Click ¡§industry¡¨ label on the screen at the left side, the lower left side will display tree nodes of all industries and customers not set up with industry types.

According to the selected industry type node, the screen on the right side will display released customer data in this industry and newly added and unreleased customer data list.

Click the node of customer not set up with industry category, the screen on the right side will then display released customer data and newly added unreleased customer data list for customer not set up with industry category.

Note: The released customer data displayed on the screen are customer data that an individual has the authority to browse.

Query by region

Query of customer data by region

Click ¡§region¡¨ label on the screen at the left side, the lower left side will then display tree nodes of customers of all regional categories and customers not set up with regional category.

According to the selected regional category node, the screen on the right side will display released customer data and newly added unreleased customer data list in this regional category.

When you click regional category father node, the displayed customer data will include customer data of sub-node of regional category under it.

When you click node of customer not set up with regional category, the screen on the right side will display released customer data and newly added unreleased customer data list not set up with regional category.

Note: The released customer data displayed on the screen are customer data that an individual has the authority to browse.
