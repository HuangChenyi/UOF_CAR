Loan management

Keeper can manage on the resource and make on load, reject or return to manage resource of the company.

Check the loaning situation according to time
Click first ¡§Loan Mgt.¡¨ of ¡§Booking Mgt.¡¨, then click ¡§By time¡¨.
System will then display calendar of that month and show the record of the loan status of the resource. Different resource keeper will see different resource item respectively.
Click the loaned item to set up the loaning status of the resource. The status can be divided into three types, namely, ¡§On load¡¨, ¡§Reject¡¨ and ¡§Return¡¨. When the borrower indeed borrow out the resource, the keeper then records it as ¡§On load¡¨; the keeper, due to certain situation and trouble on the resource, can reject the borrower; when the borrower returns, the keeper then records it as ¡§Return¡¨.
If the keeper wants to see singly certain resource or category, the keeper can click the resource name or category name of ¡§Category list¡¨at the left side.
