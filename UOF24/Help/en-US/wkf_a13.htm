Use form template to set up new form

It is built in with hundreds of default forms, and you can edit the field of the form template by yourself.

You can go to the “Form Design” to select the form to be set up, then click “New form reversion”.
Select the third method as the setup method: Import external form, click “Import Template”, and you can use hundreds of form templates provided by the system, starting in sequence from form category to form name, you can check the fields you need, then click “Submit” to finish.
During the designing of the form field, you can also add directly the fields you need directly from form templates set up already. Click “Select form template”, similarly, start in sequence from form category and form name to check the fields you need, then click “Submit” to finish.
The checked fields will be added in the form directly.
