Applied Form

It is to check the form submitted for review. If the application is in mistake, the form can be checked out before it is reviewed by the approver, or the applicant can follow up the form if the approval form is still not approved by the approver for a long time. Besides, you can also view or print the form.

For the application form status, please select ¡§in process¡¨, the system will then list the forms under processing.

Note: Form checked out by the applicant: Before the form is closed, it can be directly checked out.
