How to browse and subscribe the notification

Browse notification
After entering notification, the left side is ¡§Notification category list¡¨, and the right side is the list of the most updated notification.
If you want to search all kinds of notifications, click first the notification category, then the notification of the category will appear at the right side of the screen, you will see the subject, posted by, release time and browse record (only the manager can see the browsing record); the user can add the notification to the collection.
Click directly on the notification subject to read notification, and you can then see the content and attachment of notification. If it can be printed, ¡§Print¡¨ button will be visible on the screen, you can click it to print notification content.
