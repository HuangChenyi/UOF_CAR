Field control description
Not required for Applicant: When the user is applying the form, this field is allowed not to enter.
Required for Applicant: When the user is applying the form, this field must be entered.
Input by Specified members: This field is allowed to be entered only by certain assignedmember, and othermember are not allowed to enter data.
Required for specified members: This field must be entered for certainmember, but othermember are not allowed to enter.
External information: You can edit web service program to get the external data, for example: ERP or accounting, financing, or human resource system, etc.
Modify authority level
This function can be used to set up who has the authority to change this field (filled).
Inherit form: This field inherits the authority of modify of form, and the authority of modify of form can be set up at ¡§Maintain form¡¨.
Modification not allowed: Anyone is not allowed to modify this field.
Modification allowed: Only certain assignedmember are allowed to modify this field.

Setup multi-language of field
Since the same form might be used by people of different nationalities, for example, people across the strait or in Europe or US area use the same form for the application, hence, to meet the multiple language requirements, you can enter different field texts for different languages.
Click ¡§Setup multi-languages of field¡¨ , you can set up system texts of English, traditional Chinese and simplified Chinese, and click ¡§Activate¡¨to start the setup. You can click ¡§Preview¡¨ to view the effect.
After the setup, when people of different languages are logging in and are applying different forms, they can see form of their own language.
