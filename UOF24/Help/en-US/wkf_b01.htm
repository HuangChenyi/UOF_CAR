Sub-flow design

Maintain Category
Category must be set up first before sub-flow, click “maintain category” to enter editing screen, then click “New category” and enter category name.
New sub-flow
Click category of sub-flow, then click “New sub-flow” , then enter the name of the flow.
The setup initiation basis can be divided into three types, “applicant”, “Department and Position of previous stop”, and “By below setup”. However, if you want to select startingmember by yourself, you can click “Select Department” and “Select Position” to select. After finishing it, please click “Save information”.
Start setting up site, click “New stop”. And sub-flow provides the following three types of sites, which are described as in the followings:
1. “Approval stop”
Specify by the previous stop: That is, the approvalmember of the next stop should be assigned by this stop.
Approval type:
  • 一般：該站點只能有一名簽核者，同意即送下一站，該簽核者否決表單即結案。
  • 並簽：該站點可設定多人簽核，一人簽核同意即送下一站，會依「表單維護」的並簽設定來決定是否一人否決即可結案。
  • 會簽：該站點可設定多人簽核，全部簽核者同意才送下一站，一位簽核者否決即結案。
Can this site be modified: Approval form can be changed; approvalmember can also be changed, added or reduced.
Delay setup: Each site can be set up with time-out time, if approval is not finished when time is up, the system will follow up the approver.
2. “Customized stop”
The user, during the progressing of the form, can self-make approval flow.
3. “Organization stop”
Approval type:
  • 一般：該站點只能有一名簽核者，同意即送下一站，該簽核者否決表單即結案。
  • 並簽：該站點可設定多人簽核，一人簽核同意即送下一站，會依「表單維護」的並簽設定來決定是否一人否決即可結案。
  • 會簽：該站點可設定多人簽核，全部簽核者同意才送下一站，一位簽核者否決即結案。
Can this stop be modified: Approval form can be changed; approvalmember can also be changed, added or reduced; or you can select whether it should be sent down to the next site or not, or you can select to close the case to stop this sub-flow.
Delay setup: Each site can be set up with time-out time, if approval is not finished when time is up, the system will follow up the approver.
Approver (Field ID): Yu can follow the selected department,member, position or job title to be used as flow judgment. Then enter the field ID, and follow the selected content on the form as the approver of the stop.
Approver: In approval, you can select “Specific approval” or “Normal approval” approver, and the setup method is as in the following:
1. “Specific approval”
Applicant: Approver is the applicant of this form.
Applicant’s supervisor: Approver is the department supervisor of the applicant, and the job title should be checked the department supervisor.
Previous stop’s substitute: Approver is the substitute person of the previous stop, which is commonly used in the leave form.
Previous stop’s direct supervisor: Approver is the department supervisor of the previous site.
2. “Normal approval”
To select directly the approver, click “Select member” , then check approver, you can select multi-person approval, and you can also select member according to department, position, function, department+ position or department+ function; you can click “Preview” to view ifmember are correct, then click “Submit” to finish.
Notify member: members that want to be notified before approval or after approval. You can select “Special notice” or “Normal notice”, and the setup method is as in the following:
1. “Special notice”
Applicant: When the notification person is the applicant of this form.
Direct supervisor of the applicant: When the notification person is the department supervisor of the applicant, and the job title should be checked with department director.
The substitute person of the previous site: Notification person is the substitute person of the pervious site.
Direct supervisor of the previous site: Notification person is the department supervisor of the previous site.
2. “Normal notice”
To select directlymember to be notified, please click “select member”, then click themember to be notified. You can select to notify multi-person, or you can select member according to department, position, function, department + position or department+ function. You can click “preview” to view ifmember are OK, then click “Submit” to finish.

Note: The default sub-flow of the system contains free flow and blank flow, free flow is sub-flow that the user decides the approver of the next stop, blank flow means to skip the flow to go to the next stop.
