Batch Leave

Administrator and Leave Administrator can make group Leave applications for employees without Apply the Leave Form, employees can query in Leave records.

Select Start & End time, Leave, members, input hours(days) and reason, then click ¡iGenerate Batch Leave Detail¡j, it will generate a new Batch Leave record after validation successful or display a failure message after validation failure, validation rules are shown below:
Required field cannot be blank.
Time(End) must be later than Time(Start).
The Leave is not enable.
The available hours/days is not enough.
The Batch Leave is already existed.
Time of Batch Leave overlaps the time of other Leave/Overtime.
Remark: Time(Start/End) and hours of Batch Leave is only for reference, it will not compare with Shift.
The Batch Leave is not enable after generate Batch Leave detail, click ¡iConfirm Batch Leave¡jto enable the Batch Leave if the validation is correct. You can modify the data of individual member by click ¡iDetail¡j, search member and modify Leave, hours/days, or even delete the record. You can also click [Export] to get a Excel file of all detail.
Click ¡iConfirm¡jafter modification or ¡iConfirm Batch Leave¡j, system will validate all rules.
The checkbox of member is for deletion not for ¡iConfirm Batch Leave¡j, All members will enable Batch Leave after click ¡iConfirm Batch Leave¡jno matter the checkbox is checked or not.
If Batch Leave enabled, the details can't be modified, if the data is error, Leave Administrator can delete the record and re-generate, this Batch Leave will be shown as "Void" in members Leave records.
