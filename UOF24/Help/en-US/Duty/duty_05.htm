How to maintain annual leave (days) of configuration

You can set up the number of annual leave earned(days) corresponding to the seniority. It can be unified for the entire company or it can be set up according to different department (branch office).

Add and maintain annual leave
When the duty module is introduced, accumulated years of experience of 30 years that is defaulted to the system will be brought up automatically.
