Search method of approval statistics

It is to search approval statistics of certain month. Duty administrator can also specify approval or follow-up.

The monthly related quantity of the applied duty form will all be displayed this searched list. However, if there is still certain quantity in the duty form not finished with approval, you will be unable to do settlement and closure action for that period.
If there are forms not approved, then the total unfinished quantity of form will be displayed in the field of ¡§Number of Unfinished Approval¡¨. Click the unfinished quantity, you can then open ¡§Unfinished Approval¡¨ window, then you can follow up each form waiting for approval or make specify approval action.
