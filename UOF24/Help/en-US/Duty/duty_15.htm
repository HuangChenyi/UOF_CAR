Search method of leave history

It lets an individual be able to search his leave history, and it also lets department manager be able to search the status of the duty leave form of each department and view the form content.

Query and view
If the user does not have the authority of the department administrator, he can only view his leave history.
Department administrator can search the status of the duty leave form of each department.
For the searched list, ¡§Export¡¨ can be used to export the list into Excel file.
Click the form No. to open the printing window of the form to view.
When there is department switch in personnel, supervisor of the old department will be unable to see the leave form of the member after he is switched to the new department; but the supervisor of the new department can see the leave data of the member in all the historical departments.
