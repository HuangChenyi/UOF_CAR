Search method of overtime history

It lets an individual be able to search his overtime history, and it also lets department administrator be able to search the status of the duty leave form of each department and view the form content too.

Query and view
If the user does not have the authority of the department administrator, he can then only see his own overtime history.
Department administrator can search the status of the duty overtime history of each department.
For the searched list, you can use ¡§Export¡¨ to export the list into Excel file.
Click overtime form number to open the printing window of the form for viewing.
When there is department switch in personnel, supervisor of the old department will be unable to see the overtime data of the member after he is switched to the new department; but the supervisor of the new department can see the overtime data of the member in all the historical departments.
