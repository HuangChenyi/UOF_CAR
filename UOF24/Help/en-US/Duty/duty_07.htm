The annual leave of this year is generated according to the date employee took office of the employee and the setup reservation of last year.

Import annual leave information

Click ¡§Import¡¨ button to open ¡§Import Annual Leave Information¡¨ window. Download first Excel template, after finishing the maintenance, please perform the uploading and import action.
If the generated department annual leave information is to be adjusted, you can also use this annual leave information import function to update the originally existed annual leave information.
When the system already has the annual leave information of the newer year, the annual leave information of old year will be unable to be generated again. (For example: If the annual leave information of 2012 has been generated already, then the annual leave information of 2011will be unable to be generated again.)

If the annual leave information of certain member is to be maintained directly, you can click name of the member and open ¡§Maintain Annual leave¡¨ window to perform the maintenance. After finishing, please press ¡§Submit¡¨ button, then the annual leave information of the member will then be updated immediately into the revised content.
