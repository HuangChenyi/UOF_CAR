Administrator setup

Administrator of different characters, within different modules, owns different authority. For the detail of authority, please refer to the following description.

BPM Administrator: BPM authority management, form maintenance, flow maintenance and form management.

System Slogan Admin: Corporate Motto maintenance.

System administrator: Organization maintenance, system configuration setup and authorization management.

SMS administrator: SMS authority management, SMS sending and SMS search.

 Questionnaire administrator: Questionnaire authority management, questionnaire maintenance and questionnaire statistics.

 Project administrator: Project authority management, project maintenance and project search.

 Equipment administrator:  Category management, equipment management, loaning management, adding of new loaning, change of loaning, deletion of loaning and browsing of loaning record.

Video administrator: Video category authority maintenance, video maintenance and the viewing of video.

Forum administrator:  Forum setup, forum administrator setup, discussion board setup, board manager setup, viewable member setup, Digest setting, highlighted topic, lock up topic, deletion of article and modification of article.

Official Letter Administrator : Allow to query Official Letters and export to Excel files in [BPM > Official Letter Query]

Duty administrator: Basic data maintenance, settlement, leave statistics search.

Document administrator: Category/directory/document maintenance, document BPM maintenance and the directory authority setup.

Announcement administrator: Category management, management of poster, deletion of notification, post of notification, modification of notification and browsing of notification.

Album manager: Album category maintenance, album authority setup, album maintenance, and viewing of album.
