How to call for a meeting

Every person can call for a meeting to invite the colleagues to join, and he can also borrow the equipment needed and prepare all the people, things and place and object needed for a meeting.

There are three ways: 1. Call for a meeting directly, 2. Proceed with inquiry, 3. Call meeting from all inquired. Please pick up one of them.

Editing screen will appear, please select meeting ¡§chair¡¨ first, then select the joining members, then click ¡§select member¡¨, then check the department or member, then click ¡§Submit¡¨ .

Enter ¡§subject¡¨ and ¡§description¡¨ of the meeting, then select ¡§start and end time¡¨ or ¡§meeting contact person¡¨, if you want to perform inquiry first, you will need to set up ¡§planned meeting time¡¨ and ¡§inquiry deadline¡¨ ,  after all the inquiries have been received, you can click ¡§query inquiry result¡¨ to check the opinions from all members.

Meeting place: The selection items can be divided into: online meeting, book meeting room and other rooms.

 

  • Online meeting: This selection item will appear only when you need to activate the online meeting. For the related setup, please refer to General setup-module setup

 

  • Meeting room loaning: When there is new loaning, if the selected resource is meeting room, then the system will display the resource name automatically as the name of the meeting room, the user cannot enter at his side.

  • Note:

    1. If you loan for more than two meeting room, the name will be separated by semicolon.

    2. For the setup method of the resource type, please refer to Resource management.

 

  • Other rooms: The user can enter the room himself.

 

Display: The open status can be freely selected, that is, the display status when others are checking my calendar, for example: Display as busy or display as out.

 

Attachment: When you are to convene a meeting, you can attach with an attachment so that the users can understand related items of the meeting.

 

Reminder time: You can set up the system to send mail notification so that a reminder can be sent out a few minutes before the meeting.
