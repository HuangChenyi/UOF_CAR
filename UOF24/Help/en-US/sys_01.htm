Internal staff

It is to manage the internal department and members of the company and to maintain the organization structure of the company. Tree structure is used to set up the subordinate relationship among departments; for the member part, you can set up the job title, function, educational background and specialty of the member, and the system administrator will be responsible for the planning and maintaining the department and for managing the members.
