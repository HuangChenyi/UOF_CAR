Query by supervisor

Department supervisor can, for special form, query the form application information for all themember the supervisor manages. You can also view or print the form.

Query of form

On the form list at the left side, click the name of the form to be queried, then select the apply datetime and department information to be queried (which can include sub-department).

Enter Form No., Subject, Applicant and Content for keyword query.

View and print the form

Supervisor can check the form information applied by the subordinate and its approval status. Moreover, the supervisor can print the form result, and if it is set up with background picture, the background picture situation can be seen during the printing.
