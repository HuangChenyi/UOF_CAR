Fill in form by others

You can inquire the forms you apply for others, and you can view or print the form.

Inquire forms you apply for others

Forms apply for others mean the person who writes and sends out the form on behalf of others can inquire the flow and processing situation of the form.

The form status can be divided into: all status, in process, closed (not including voided forms), abnormal and void form. The applicant can follow these five statuses together with the inquiry date to inquire the related forms.

View and print applied form

The user can check the situation of the forms waiting for approval, or check the approval result of the closed form. Meanwhile, the user can also print the form result, and if it is set up with background picture, you will be able to see the background picture during the printing.
