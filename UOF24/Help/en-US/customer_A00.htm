Authority setup

You can set up authority of themember, and the authorities available for setup include: data manager, new data provider, data modifier and data remover.

Set up role authority formember

Select the role in the role list, then click ¡§select member¡¨ button to entermember selection screen, the selectedmember will then own the authority of the role.

Role description

New data provider

Add new not released customer data and contact person data, and release newly added customer data.

Add contact person data to the released customer data. However, the released customer data and contact person data cannot be modified or deleted.

Data modifier

Add new not released customer data and contact person data, and release newly added customer data.

Modify the released customer data, and add and modify the contact person data, however, the released customer data and contact person data cannot be deleted.

Data remover: This person can add, modify and remove released customer data and contact person data.

Add not released customer data and contact person data, and release newly added customer data.

Modify and remove released customer data and add, modify or delete contact person data.

Data manager: This person owns maximum authority to maintain main file, customer data, contact person data and to view the event.

Add new not released customer data and contact person data, and release newly added customer data.

Modify and delete released customer data, and add, modify or delete contact person data.
