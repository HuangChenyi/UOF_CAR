Approval Form

It is to check the approval forms. If the approval is in mistake, you can check out the form before it is approved by approver in the next site. Or if the approver does not approve it for a long time, you can follow up the form. In addition, you can also view or print the form.

Query the singed forms

approval forms mean that the flow and handling situation for form query after the approver has sent out the form.

Form status can be divided into all status, in process, closed, abnormal and void. The approver can follow these five statuses together with the application date to query related forms.
