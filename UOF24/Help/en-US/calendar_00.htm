Calendar

It is a site belongs specifically to a person. You can record and plan personal work schedule and progress, or record the memorandum content to remind yourself some important items.

You can hold a meeting and record the meeting result, and you can assign the meeting conclusions to related member for handling. Anymember invited for the meeting can reply for joining or refusing so that the meeting convener can know who are going to join the meeting.

You can set up the off-duty date in the calendar so that the director and colleagues can check it.

The director or colleagues can assign the task to othermember and search the situation and progress.
