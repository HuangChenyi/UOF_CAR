Maintain video

系統管理員/影音管理員可將欲分享的影片放置「影音專區」。可從系統管理的「系統管理→系統組態→一般組態設定→模組設定」去設定幾天內上傳的影片要顯示NEW字樣。

Maintain category
Click first “Maintain Video” of “Video”, then click “Maintain Category”.
Next, click “New category”, then enter the “name” of the category, after the setup, press “Exit” to finish.
 
 
Maintain category authority
Click certain setup category, then click “Maintain Category Authority”.
Next, select member who are allowed to browse this category, after the selection, click “Submit” to finish. The selected members will be the view member of this video category.
